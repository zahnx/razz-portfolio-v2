zydays.a - Design Process

This project marks my first client-based Webflow project and was smooth and straightforward. After receiving the project brief, I gathered inspiration from various platforms and created a mood board to ensure the design aligned with the client’s preferred look and feel. I then developed a wireframe to outline the website structure, followed by a detailed UI design for the client to visualize the final product. Once the design was approved, I implemented the website in Webflow, making it user-friendly and responsive. Additionally, I integrated a CMS for easy management of their affiliated products.
